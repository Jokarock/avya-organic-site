Our Story

Rooted in nature,
built with purpose

AVYA was born from a deeply personal conviction — that the way we care for ourselves should mirror how we choose to live: naturally, purely, and in harmony with the world around us.

Our bath collection is crafted by artisans who have shaped bamboo, sisal, and beechwood for generations. Every brush is designed to be used with intention, cherished through daily ritual, and eventually returned to the earth. Pure materials from root to finish — no microplastics, no synthetics, no compromise.

Our bag collection honours India’s extraordinary craft heritage — hand-block printed cotton, naturally dyed hemp, and hand-knotted macramé, made by skilled artisan communities using techniques carried across generations. Each piece carries the fingerprint of the hands that made it.

Two collections. One conviction: live more naturally, carry fewer synthetics, and leave a lighter, more beautiful mark on this earth.

What We Stand For

Our Commitments

🌿
Pure by Nature
No synthetics, no shortcuts. Every material is chosen for its natural origin — because living naturally starts with what touches your skin.
🌿
Bamboo First
Fast-growing, carbon-sequestering, and naturally antibacterial. Bamboo is how we bring the forest into your morning ritual.
🤝
Artisan Heritage
Every bag traces its origin to skilled hands in India using techniques carried forward across generations. Craft, not manufacture.
♻
Light Footprint
Everything from material to packaging is chosen to leave the smallest possible mark. Pure living means caring for the earth, not just yourself.
Sourced · Southeast Asia
Moso Bamboo
The fastest-growing plant on earth. Our bamboo handles are harvested from sustainably managed forests, laser-etched with our branding, and naturally antibacterial. Each handle is unique — no two have the same grain.
Sourced · Mexico & Africa
Natural Sisal
Harvested from the agave plant, sisal is one of the world’s strongest natural fibres. Our sisal bristles provide firm, effective exfoliation that improves circulation and removes dead skin — without a single gram of plastic.
Crafted · Rajasthan, India
Cotton & Hemp
Our bags are made from unbleached cotton canvas and raw hemp fibre sourced from small-scale Indian farms. Block-printing is done by hand using vegetable dyes — each print slightly different, each bag genuinely one of a kind.
